About Us

We are a small start-up company looking to advertise our product. Based in Salt Lake City, we had initially partnered up with Smith's to create a newly enhanced scanner to decrease scanning times with products. While this idea never came to fruition, it was from this collaboration that our book reader was conceived.

Our sales have been steadily growing, and we are seeking to expand to other continents. With increasing sales comes a greater worldwide reputation, which eventually allows us to develop larger projects.
